About Me

I started this site in 2018 while I was studying psychology at IMSU. Since I came from a low-income family, the only way I could afford college was to pay my own way. That’s when I started seeking other ways of earning an income while I studied. That is how my journey with software began, leading me to where I am today. But when I got my first paycheck, I invested it in my workstation at home to set it up for optimum productivity…and immediately went broke. Oops. But the satisfaction of seeing my work come to life is what still leads me to this day.

Work Space and Ethics

Remember the workstation I alluded to earlier on? well here it is and it is from here that I reaching you now, I spend 90% of my waking hours here on that chair and desk, I have developed a work ethic unrivaled in the industry, waking up by 5: 40 AM every day, I say a morning prayer and step out to walk my dogs, (3 Boerboel puppies) we take a brisk walk for 30 minutes to 1 hour and I am back at home by 7:00 AM I freshen up to grab a cup of coffee and get to work on the task of the day, only standing up again midday to grab lunch and I could go all day till evening like clock-work every day. I have come to realize that I am Dependable, Reliable, committed, and dedicated.
